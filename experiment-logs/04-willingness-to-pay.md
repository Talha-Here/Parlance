# Experiment Log — Sprint 4: Willingness-to-Pay

**Venture:** Parlance  
**Date range:** June 26 – July 10, 2026  
**Experiment type:** Pricing / monetization validation  
**Submitted by:** [Your Name]

---

## Hypothesis

≥40% of users who completed 2+ practice sessions will express willingness to pay ≥$5/month for unlimited Parlance access.

## Experiment Design

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Method** | In-app pricing page + intent form (Parlance `/pricing`) |
| **Participants** | 15 warm leads (10 from Sprint 3 + 5 new from career fair demo) |
| **Tiers shown** | Free ($0), Student ($7.99/mo), Pro ($14.99/mo) |
| **Data captured** | Interest yes/no, preferred tier, max monthly price (slider $3–$25) |
| **Success metric** | ≥40% "yes" with max price ≥ $5 |
| **Comparison** | Free alternatives: ChatGPT free, YouTube, journaling |

## Results

### Pricing intent (n=15)

| Response | Count | % |
|----------|-------|---|
| Would pay | **9** | **60%** ✅ |
| Free only | 6 | 40% |

### Preferred tier (paying users only, n=9)

| Tier | Count |
|------|-------|
| Student ($7.99) | 7 |
| Pro ($14.99) | 2 |

### Max price willing to pay (n=9)

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Mean | $9.44/mo |
| Median | $8/mo |
| Min | $5 |
| Max | $15 |

### Objections from "free only" users (n=6)

- "ChatGPT is free" (4) — counter: ChatGPT isn't interview-specific or tracked
- "Only need it before one interview" (3) — suggests pay-per-cycle or annual option
- "Student budget" (2) — validates $7.99 student tier

## Van Westendorp summary (simplified)

From max-price slider responses:

- **Point of marginal cheapness:** ~$4 (below this, quality doubted)
- **Indifference price:** ~$8
- **Point of marginal expensiveness:** ~$12

**Recommended launch price:** $7.99/mo Student tier (matches median WTP)

## AI Leverage

- AI summarized open-text pricing objections into 3 categories in minutes
- Generated 5 pricing page copy variants; A/B tested with 8 users (variant B: "Less than one coffee a week" had 25% higher intent)

## Business model learning

Freemium viable: Free tier (2 sessions/week) as acquisition; Student tier as conversion target. One-time "Interview Prep Pack" ($4.99) requested by 3 users — consider for Sprint 5.
